Tajwid Learning Center

Master the rules of precise and beautiful Quranic recitation

1. Makharij Huruf

The correct emission points for every single Arabic letter from the mouth and throat regions.

2. Sifat Huruf

The intrinsic characteristics and qualities of letters (such as breath containment or echo features).

3. Nun Sakinah & Tanwin

Rules governing silent Nun and double vowels, covering Izhar, Idgham, Iqlab, and Ikhfa.

4. Mim Sakinah

Rules governing unvowelled Mim when followed by other letters (Ikhfa Syafawi, Idgham Syafawi, Izhar Syafawi).

5. Rules of Mad

Understanding vowel elongation rules, separating foundational natural elongation from secondary branches.

6. Qalqalah & Ghunnah

Mastering the clear echoing/bouncing sounds and nasalization rules for deep phonetic accuracy.

7. Waqaf & Ibtida

The science of knowing where to safely stop during long recitations and how to correctly resume without breaking meaning.

8. Common Mistakes

A clear compilation highlighting frequent pronunciation pitfalls, mixed-up letters, and vowel measurement errors.
