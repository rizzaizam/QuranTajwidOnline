Pronunciation Audio Samples

Listen to the correct pronunciation of complex Makharij (emission points) and Tajwid rules. Repeat after the reciter to practice.

1. Al-Halq (The Throat)
Makharij

Listen to the pronunciation of letters originating from the throat: ع, غ, ح, خ, هـ, ء

2. Qalqalah (The Echo)
Tajwid Rule

Notice the bouncing or echoing sound when pronouncing Sakinah letters: ق, ط, ب, ج, د

3. Ghunnah (Nasalization)
Tajwid Rule

Listen to the 2-harakat nasal sound produced for Nun and Mim with a Shaddah (ّ ).

4. Heavy vs Light Letters
Makharij

Compare the heavy (Tafkhim) letter 'ص' with the light (Tarqiq) letter 'س'.
